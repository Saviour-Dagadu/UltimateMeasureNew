Saviour Dagadu  

IT/ Survey Manager

Saviour has over 10 years of diverse experience in the research industry and is currently a Software Engineer. He is skilled and competent in data collection, processing, management, project planning, monitoring and evaluation.

Over the years he has developed capacity in qualitative and quantitative analysis, database development and data collection Apps development, building of data tools, facilitation of training, report writing and communication skills with outstanding interpersonal abilities.

He has worked with several international and local organizations on diverse projects including USAID, Innovations for poverty action(IPA), International Cocoa Initiative(ICI), ISSER (University of Ghana), Sal Consult Limited, IVY League Consult, JMK Consult amongst others.

His experience cuts across sectors such as health, agriculture, education, banking and finance, democracy and governance, electrical energy, rural/urban development and resettlements, social welfare, and children/women protection.

Saviour holds a Bachelors of Science degree in Mathematics and Business Management, an Associate degree in Software Engineering, as well as other academic background in Purchasing and Supply Chain Management, Project Management, Credit Risk Management and Oil and gas.
